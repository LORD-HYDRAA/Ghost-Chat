Usage Examples:
Host a chat:

bash
./run.sh -host -key "myOuterKey123" -inner "myInnerKey456" -chat "myChatKey789" -username "Alice"
Join a chat:

bash
./run.sh -join -key "myOuterKey123" -inner "myInnerKey456" -chat "myChatKey789" -username "Bob" -address "abc123def456.onion"
